## Summary  
Orbach explains how post structure and formatting impact readability and virality on LinkedIn. He emphasizes rhythm, sentence variation, and visual flow as key elements of engaging content.

## Key Insight  
- Content structure significantly affects readability and engagement  
- Alternating sentence length creates rhythm that keeps attention  
- Visual formatting influences how users consume content  
- Iterative editing improves content clarity and performance  
- Writing for attention requires both message and presentation optimization  

## Deeper Interpretation  
This post focuses on the mechanics of content consumption rather than messaging itself. Orbach frames virality as a function of cognitive flow — how the human eye processes structured text. The underlying idea is that effective content is not only about what is said, but how it is visually and rhythmically delivered.

## Takeaway  
Content performance is influenced not only by ideas, but by formatting, rhythm, and visual readability.
