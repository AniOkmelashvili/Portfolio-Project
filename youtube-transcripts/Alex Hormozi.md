# Alex Hormozi - YouTube Video

## Video Link
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p5u28z1FAsI

## Topic
How to Create Irresistible Offers

## Summary
Alex Hormozi explains that the core of business success is making offers that people feel “stupid saying no to.” He defines an offer as the terms of exchange between a business and a customer.

He breaks down value into four key variables and explains how businesses can increase perceived value while reducing friction. By improving these variables, companies can charge higher prices, increase conversions, and grow faster.

## Key Insights

- An offer is the foundation of sales and business growth  
- The goal is to create an offer that is extremely hard to refuse  
- Value is defined by four main variables:
  - Dream outcome (what the customer wants)
  - Perceived likelihood of success  
  - Time delay (how fast results come)  
  - Effort and sacrifice required  
- Higher perceived success = higher willingness to buy  
- Faster results increase value significantly  
- Less effort and sacrifice increases attractiveness  
- Better targeting (specific audience) increases perceived value  
- Businesses can increase price by improving the offer, not just marketing  

## Key Quote Idea
“Make people an offer they’d feel stupid saying no to.”

## Takeaway
The most important lever in business is improving your offer. When value is high and friction is low, sales become much easier and more profitable.
