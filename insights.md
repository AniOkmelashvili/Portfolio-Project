# Core Insight

Offer + People + Easy path to buy

## Insight

Business is not about how much content you post.

Simply:
- If your offer is bad → people won’t buy  
- If people don’t see you → no one comes  
- If buying is complicated → you lose money  

## How it works

1. Sell something good (offer)  
2. Get people to see it (attention)  
3. Make it easy to buy (simple funnel)  

## Simple Formula

Content → Profile → Offer → Buy 
## Takeaway

You don’t need more content.  
You need:
- a better offer  
- more attention  
- and an easy way for people to buy
