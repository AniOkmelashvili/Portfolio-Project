Project Update – Step 2: Expert Deep Research & Content Collection

LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

## Project Overview
This project explores how B2B SaaS companies grow using LinkedIn organic content.
The goal is to collect high-quality insights from real practitioners and organize them into a structured research repository.

## Why I chose this topic
I chose LinkedIn organic content because it is one of the most effective and accessible growth channels for B2B SaaS companies.
It also aligns with my background in outreach and lead generation.

## What I did
•	Selected 10 high-quality experts who actively share insights on LinkedIn and YouTube
•	Collected their recent LinkedIn posts and video transcripts
•	Organized all materials into structured folders inside the repository

## What I learned
•	Consistency and personal branding are key drivers of LinkedIn growth
•	High-performing posts often follow simple, repeatable frameworks
•	Content that shares real experience performs better than generic advice

•	Cursor IDE
•	Claude Code
•	Codex
•	GitHub

•	/research/sources.md — list of selected experts
•	/research/linkedin-posts/ — collected LinkedIn posts
